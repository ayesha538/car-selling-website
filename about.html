About Ayvenza Motors

Redefining automotive excellence through passion, innovation, and unparalleled customer experiences.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Ayvenza Motors began as a small boutique dealership with a vision to bring the world's most exclusive vehicles to discerning collectors and enthusiasts.

Our founder, Alexander Venza, a lifelong car enthusiast and former racing driver, recognized a gap in the market for a truly personalized luxury car buying experience.

Today, with showrooms in Beverly Hills, Miami, and New York, Ayvenza Motors continues to set the standard for excellence in the premium automotive sector.
